Cats are small, carnivorous mammals that are often kept as pets. They are known for their agility, playfulness, and ability to hunt vermin. Cats have been domesticated for thousands of years and are one of the most popular pets in the world.

Here are some interesting facts about cats:
1. Cats have excellent night vision and can see in light levels six times lower than what a human needs.
2. A group of cats is called a clowder, and a group of kittens is called a kindle.
3. Cats have a specialized collarbone that allows them to always land on their feet when they fall.
4. The average cat sleeps for 12-16 hours a day.
5. Cats communicate with humans through a variety of vocalizations, including purring, meowing, and hissing.

Cats are fascinating creatures that bring joy and companionship to millions of people around the world.
